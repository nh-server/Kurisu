Loadiine should never be used because.....
- Severly outdated
- Slow
- Buggy
- Lags
- Has graphical glitches
- Lacks online play
- Kills SD cards

The better and recommended way is to dump and install your game discs to the homescreen. Try `.dump wiiu` in <#261581918653513729>.
